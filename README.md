# 404devs

> Developer Forum — a free space for comfortable communication and knowledge growth.

---

## About the Project

**404 dev** is not just another forum.  
We are building a **friendly, structured, and accessible community** for developers who are tired of toxicity, spam, and outdated platforms.

The project is built on one simple idea:  
> "Here, you're not afraid to ask a question."

No judgment for "stupid" mistakes. Just real people ready to help, a comfortable atmosphere, and a convenient knowledge base that grows alongside you.
